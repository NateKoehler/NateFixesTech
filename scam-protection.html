What I Can Help With

  • Review suspicious messages and calls
  • Lock down phones, computers, and email accounts
  • Set safer passwords and account recovery options
  • Teach family members what warning signs to watch for
  • Create a simple fraud response plan for emergencies

Common Scam Warning Signs

  • Pressure to act right now
  • Requests for gift cards, wire transfers, or crypto
  • Caller says "do not tell anyone"
  • Links that ask you to "verify" account details
  • Tech support popups claiming your computer is infected

If You Think a Scam Happened

  • Stop communication immediately
  • Call your bank or card provider right away
  • Change passwords on important accounts
  • Report identity theft or fraud to official agencies
  • Contact me for immediate cleanup and device checks

You are not alone. Fast action can reduce damage and help protect the next person.
